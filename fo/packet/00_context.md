# Company Context

- The company is a fast-growing B2B SaaS business serving enterprise customers in a regulated industry.
- Customers care about security, uptime, responsiveness, and implementation reliability.
- The company is still founder-led and moves quickly, but a lot of important work crosses functions and can drift without clear ownership.
- The founder is traveling today and has limited availability.
- Outside counsel is available, but usually slow unless given a concise summary and focused asks.
- The company is in the middle of a SOC 2 evidence review with an external consultant.
- Hiring for leadership roles is active because the company is scaling quickly.
- HR is supported, but the founder is still involved in some sensitive decisions.
