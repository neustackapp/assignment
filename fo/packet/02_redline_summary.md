# Redline Summary

Below is the commercial summary prepared by the founder late Monday night.

## 1. Indemnity
Customer asks for broader indemnification language than the company standard, including language that could be read to cover more than third-party claims.

## 2. Uptime
Customer proposes 99.95% uptime commitment. Company standard language is lower and includes broader carve-outs.

## 3. Data retention / deletion
Customer requests deletion of data within a fixed timeline after termination, plus a custom retention commitment aligned to their policy.

## 4. Support SLAs
Customer requests explicit response-time SLAs for severity levels 1 through 4.

## Notes
- No one has yet decided where the company has flexibility and where it does not.
- Outside counsel knows the general form but has not seen this latest draft.
- Business sponsor appears supportive, but procurement is watching pace closely.
